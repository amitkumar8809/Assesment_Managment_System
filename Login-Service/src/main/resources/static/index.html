Welcome to Employee Assessment Management System

Your one-stop solution for managing technical and behavioral assessments efficiently. Whether you're an administrator overseeing employee performance or an employee looking to register for assessments, our platform simplifies the entire process.

With real-time data tracking, customizable assessment categories, and automated reporting, we aim to provide a seamless experience for users at all levels. The system supports both **technical** and **behavioral** assessments, ensuring comprehensive evaluations for skill development and behavioral insights.

Admins can easily view and manage all registrations, while employees can explore and register for assessments that fit their learning objectives. The system also provides secure login with role-based access, ensuring that data privacy and integrity are maintained. Join us today and streamline your assessment process!
